Installation Instructions for Plug-in:


Add to <Adobe Illustrator>/Plug-ins/Tools

(where <Adobe Illustrator> represents the path to the Adobe Illustrator folder)


OR


From within Adobe Illustrator open Preferences->Plug-ins & Scratch Disk... and choose an
additional folder that contains this plug-in.



On launching Adobe Illustrator, a tool with an arrow or line for an icon will appear
at the bottom of the tools pannel.
